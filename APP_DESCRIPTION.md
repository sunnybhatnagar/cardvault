# Card Vault — Play Store Listing

## Short Description (80 chars max)

Encrypted card storage with biometric lock. Photos, OCR, offline.

## Full Description

Card Vault keeps your credit and debit cards safely encrypted on your phone. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking — just you and your cards.

**Snap & Go**
Take a photo of your card and details are auto-filled using on-device OCR. Your camera roll is never accessed without permission.

**AES-256 Encrypted**
Every card photo is encrypted with AES-256-GCM. The database uses SQLCipher. Keys live in the Android Keystore. Nothing leaves your device.

**Biometric Lock**
Unlock with fingerprint, face, or device PIN. Configurable auto-lock keeps your cards safe when you step away. Card details are hidden from screenshots and lock-screen notifications.

**Organize Your Cards**
Group cards into custom categories. Search by name, issuer, or number. Flip between front and back photos with a 3D animation.

**Always With You**
Optional Google Drive backup syncs your encrypted data across devices. Export and restore local backups at any time.

**No Hidden Data Collection**
• No analytics SDKs
• No tracking
• No accounts required
• All processing is on-device

**Tech**
Built with Jetpack Compose and Material 3. Protected by Android Biometric API, SQLCipher, and AES-256 encryption.

**Permissions**
• Camera: for taking card photos (only on explicit action)
• Notifications: to alert you when a card is expiring
